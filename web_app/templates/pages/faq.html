We currently have two Megadolls collections.

Dolls From Around the World.

This collection is available for purchase in our online shop. The Dolls From Around the World introduce you to cultures, traditions, and lifestyles worldwide. They encourage children to learn about customs and beliefs that differ from their own. Through imaginative play, you can explore the unique differences we all have while we are connected through our humanness. Sheroes.

The Megadolls Sheroes collection is exclusively available through the purchase of a subscription. Each doll is a limited edition and is a surprise - revealed only once she arrives at your door. Sheroes have physical differences or unique characteristics. They help you gain a deeper understanding of the world around you and the suffering and experiences of others. Playing with a Megadolls Shero can encourage you to express yourself with honesty and authenticity while teaching you to show kindness and sensitivity to others.
